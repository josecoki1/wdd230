The target audience for this site will be young outdoor people who need a quick glimpse as to what the wather is outside based on their current geographic location. The personas can be summarized in the following two items:

  • Backpaking groups
  • Travelers

The scenarios could be as follows:

  1. Can I get a quick glimpse of the current weather in my geographic location?
  2. If I am going to travel to point B, can I get a weather update for the complete route based on my method of travel?
  3. Can I set an account and configure network parameters to alert me when weather in those parameters is about to impact me in my current location, my route and/or my destination?
  4. I have widgets on my phone. Can I set up and configure an auto-update widget for my weather settings?
  5. In the event of bad weather, can the page set up a route to the closest location that will not be impacted by the weather event? Route to safe haven/shelter?
